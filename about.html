Hello, I call San Antonio, Texas home but I have lived in several different places including Europe (military brat). However, Texas is my favorite state, where I was born, where my family is and where I have lived the most. I am a Texan through and through.  I am a pretty energetic person and I believe writing is therapy. After high school, I decided to join the military but after four years of Active Duty service in the United States Navy, I wanted to go back to school and be the creative soul I was meant to be. I currently hold an Associates of Arts from American Military University and I am currently at a four year institution in Texas studying as an Education major.  I love to travel, write (obviously), read and meet new people. I hope this site blesses you. I've written content that I believe can help us uplift us on this humbling adventure called life.
