Why I Love Cruises

Author: Rachel Williams, Published: 2018

Absolutely love cruises and if you're looking for an affordable, stress free vacation. I think you will too!

-Payment Plans - All inclusive (with a few exceptions) -Great destinations -Meet new people -Excellent staff

Don't knock a cruise until you try it, there are so many benefits to going on one. I don't want to sound like a travel brochure, but honestly I have traveled all over the world and cruises are my go-to vacation. When I get back from my cruise that starts at the end of the month, I will expand on this with a fresh list of packing and booking tips. This will be my fourth cruise and since it's been over a year since my last, I want a fresh idea on how to help you. I also plan to take lots of pictures and tell you about the destinations. .
